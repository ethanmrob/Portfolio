About Me

Hey there! Thanks for visiting my site! My name's Ethan, and I'm a web developer in the Atlanta area. I also have experience in photography, video editing/production, and graphic design.

I've worked on various projects since I started learning how to code in middle school. Since my first coding course in 2015 and continually since then (admittedly on and off at times), I've been able to learn so much about the wonderful world of coding, and I'm still just getting started.

I currently have certifications in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, PHP, jQuery, and Python. I have experience with Wordpress and Bootstrap, and I'm either currently learning or planning to learn React, C++, SQL.
